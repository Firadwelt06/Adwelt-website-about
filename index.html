The Person Behind Adwelt

Hi, I'm Olorunbukola.

A 24-year-old self-taught data professional from Nigeria — building solutions that turn messy, overwhelming data into decisions that actually move businesses forward.

The Origin

It started with a recruitment form.

I've always been the kind of person who needs to solve things. Give me a problem and enough details, and I'll find a way through. That trait was always there — but I didn't have a name for it yet.

Then one day, while filling out a Nigerian Navy recruitment form, I saw it listed: Database Administration. Something clicked. Not because I already knew what it was — but because it felt like me. A role built entirely around organizing information, solving structural problems, making systems work. I thought: "This is what I should be doing."

I didn't wait for a classroom. I started learning — MySQL, data analysis, automation, Python. I built real things. I broke real things. I fixed them. And somewhere in that process, Adwelt Enterprises was born.

"Give me a problem and the details — I'll find a way through."

The Journey

How I got here.

The Spark

Discovered database administration

A Navy recruitment form listed "Database Administration" as a role. That single line changed the direction of my life. I realised there was an entire profession built around what I naturally loved — organizing, structuring, and solving with data.

2
Self-Taught

Learned by building, not watching

No formal degree in data — just relentless curiosity and real projects. I taught myself MySQL, Python, Streamlit, Excel automation, and Flask by building systems that actually had to work. Failure was part of the process.

3
First Real Impact

Smart Result NG — school data at scale

My first major project: a result management platform for schools. I designed and deployed the entire back end in MySQL, built a Streamlit front end, and delivered a system capable of instant PDF generation and 24/7 remote access. The feedback: "Good job done — results came out on time this term."
